Dessert

TITLE

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time: Servings:
1

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon active, dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour (all purpose or bread flour)
  • A few drops of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or sugar(white or brown)

Instructions:

  1. Warm up water in the microwave for 15 seconds. The water should be warm, not hot.
  2. Mix yeast and sugar into the water. Then add flour and mix. Once it is slightly sticky, flour your work surface and kneed the dough until it's no longer super sticky.
  3. Pour a little bit of oil into a bowl and let dough rest until doubled in size.
  4. Once doubled in size, flour your workspace and shape the dough into a bagel. Add sugar or honey into boiling water and boil the bagel for 1 minute on each side.
  5. Bake at 425 degrees for 15-25 minutes or until golden brown.
