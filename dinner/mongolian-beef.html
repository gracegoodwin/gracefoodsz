Mongolian Beef

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time: Servings:
20 minutes 30 minutes 50 minutes 4

Ingredients:

  • olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 brown sugar
  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1/3 cup corn starch
  • udon noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Add ginger and garlic and saute. Combine soy sauce, hoisin sauce, water, and brown sugar in a bowl. Pour mixture into the saucepan and boil until slightly thick. Set aside.
  2. While sauce cooks, cut flank steak into 1/4 inch slices and cover in corn starch.
  3. Add oil to a skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Working in batches, cook meat until done, about 2 minutes.
  4. Once cooked, combine with the sauce over low heat. Serve over udon noodles or rice.
