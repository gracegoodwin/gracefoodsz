Lemon Thyme Chicken

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time: Servings:
15 minutes 50 minutes 1 hour and 5 minutes 4 - 6

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped thyme
  • 4 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 lemons, zest and juice
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons pepper
  • 4-4.5 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Whisk olive oil, thyme, garlic, the zest and juice of one lemon, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  2. Cut the remaining lemon into wedges. Add the chicken and lemon wedges to the bowl. Toss well.
  3. Dump the mixture into a rimmed baking sheet (or cast iron pan). Make sure chicken is skin side up and spaced apart evenly.
  4. Roast on the middle rack, rotating the pan halfway through until chicken is golden brown and registers at 165 degrees; about 50 minutes. Remove to a platter and sprinkle with parsley. Drizzle with juices from the pan and squeeze roasted lemon wedges on the chicken.
