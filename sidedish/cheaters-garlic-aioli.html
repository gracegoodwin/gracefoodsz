TITLE

Prep Time: "Cook" Time: Total Time: Servings:
5 minutes 15 minutes 20 minutes 8, 1 c. aioli

Ingredients:

  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard, optional
  • pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine garlic and salt in a bowl and mix until it makes a paste. Add lemon zest and juice; stir together and let sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Add mayonnaise, dijon mustard, and cracked pepper. Add more lemon juice for a thinner consistency.
