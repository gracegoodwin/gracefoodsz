Cream Cheese Pumpkin Bread

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time:
10 minutes 1 hour and 10 minutes 1 hour and 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup and 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup and 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs and 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 6 ounces of cream cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and butter a 9"x5" loaf plan. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, pumpkin pie spice baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another large bowl, combine pumpkin, 1 cup of sugar, butter, sour cream, 1 egg and yolk, and vanilla until smooth. Pour wet over dry and fold until combined.
  3. Make cream cheese filling: in a small bowl, stir together cream cheese, remaining egg, 2 tablespoons of flour, and 3 tablespoons of sugar.
  4. Transfer half the pumpkin batter in the pan. Dollop cream cheese filling on top and smooth in an even layer. Top with remianinf pumpkin butter.
  5. Bake until deeply golden and a toothpick comes out clean, about 1 hour and 5 minutes.
  6. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.
