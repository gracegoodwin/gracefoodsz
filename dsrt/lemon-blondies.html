Lemon Blondies

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time:
15 minutes 40 minutes 55 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 ounces white chocolate bar
  • 1/2 cup canola or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 medium lemons, zested
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (about half a lemon squeezed)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Lemon icing:
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Spray a 9x9-inch square pan with nonstick spray. Line the bottom and 2 sides of the pan with parchment paper. Spray again.
  2. In a small sauce pan, add the butter (cut it into cubes first) and whtie chocolate. Heat on medium-low heat until coompletely melted. Take off the heat.
  3. In a medium bowl, sift the flour, cornstarch, and salt. Set aside.
  4. Using a mixer, beat the sugar, eggs, and egg yolk on high-speed for 5 minutes. Add in the butter and white chocolate mix, oil, vanilla, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Mix on low until just combined.
  5. Add in the dry ingredients and mix on low speed. Take off the mixer and scrape the bowl with a rubber spatula. Pour the batter into the pan.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out with moist blondie crumbs. If the batter is raw, cook for 2 minutes and check again.
  7. Put the hot pan on a cooling rack and allow to cool completely.
  8. For the lemon icing: In a small bowl, mix the powdered sugar and lemon juice. Pour the icing on top of the blondies. Let icing set before cutting the brownies.
